Posted December 1, 2018

Sorry for not posting in a while! I've been quite busy thinking of ideas for posts next week.

This week, I will be posting ideas for extraterrestrial life on Neptune! The first step in making a theory for life on Neptune is making a producer. Here is a possible autograph in the ecosystem of Neptune:

The Mini Rays

These microbes would "drift" on the high speed winds. Their aerodynamic shape would slightly resemble manta rays. They would live just above the mantle, where organic compounds are common, but the tempature is not too hot. They would sythesis food from a mixture of organic molecules found in the atmosphere, like terrestrial lithotrophs. They would be capable of mitosis, which most of the gathered energy would go towards. I imagine them living being camoflauged and living in herds to protect themselves from the next step in the food chain...
